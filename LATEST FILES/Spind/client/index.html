About Us

During her school days, Rebecca has organized numerous events. In vying to secure sponsorships for events, Rebecca spent a lot of time submitting proposals and sourcing for company sponsors. Her experience inspired her to come up with the idea to start Spind. Rebecca constantly seeks new company sponsors to join as partners and works alongside Victor on the technicalities of running the platform. Victor is a rising entrepreneur who specialises in the technicalities of Spind and ensures the smooth running of the platform. Apart from the front end design, Victor manages the administrative details of the company. Having vast experience in managing people, Victor oversees the recruitment and staff management within Spind.


Our Vision

To revolutionize the sponsorship landscape by efficiently reducing search time and securing the best sponsorship deals that benefit both event organizers and sponsors, for the betterment of society.

Spind is a multi-sided platform that connects Event Organizers and Event Sponsors in Singapore.

SPIND connects event organizers and interested sponsors to find the perfect sponsor-event matches efficiently through a single proposal submission. Event organizers will be able to pair up with sponsors who share the same visions and goals as they do, improving the quality of the sponsorship. Substantial time will be saved from finding the right sponsor or event.


Join us now to sponsor events or to acquire sponsorships!

Features

One-time Proposal Submission

For every event, only one proposal submission is required to be submitted to Spind. The proposal will be reviewed by all our partners, and potential sponsors will then communicate with you using our in-house communication system.

Negotiate Your Deals

Negotiate the sponsorship terms with potential sponsors with ease using our in-house communication tool. The best part? All negotiated terms can be automatically generated into a law-binding contract to be signed by both parties!

Cloud Access

All your communication with other parties and contract details are safely stored in our cloud servers. Retrieve the information with ease anytime on any platform.

Google Connect

Connect your Google account to your Spind account or simply login with your Google account for easy access!
